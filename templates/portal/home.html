About CHESF

College of Health & Environmental Sciences Funtua

The College of Health and Environmental Sciences Funtua is dedicated to providing quality education in health and environmental sciences. Our mission is to train competent professionals who can address health challenges and environmental issues in our communities.

Located in Tudun Wada, Funtua, Katsina State, we offer a conducive learning environment with modern facilities and experienced faculty committed to academic excellence.
