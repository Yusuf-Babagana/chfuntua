About CHESF

College of Health & Environmental Sciences Funtua

The College of Health and Environmental Sciences Funtua (CHESF) is an NBTE-approved tertiary institution dedicated to producing competent, skilled, and ethical professionals in health and environmental sciences.

With accreditation from CHPRBN, WAHEB, EHCON, and JAMB, our programmes meet the highest national standards.

Located in Tudun Wada, Funtua, Katsina State, we offer a serene, conducive learning environment with modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong clinical affiliation partnerships.
