About CHESF

College of Health & Environmental Sciences Funtua

The College of Health and Environmental Sciences Funtua is dedicated to providing quality education in health and environmental sciences. Our mission is to train competent professionals who can address health challenges and environmental issues in our communities.

Located in Tudun Wada Funtua, Katsina State, we offer a conducive learning environment with modern facilities and experienced faculty members committed to academic excellence.

Our institution is recognized by relevant regulatory bodies and continues to expand its academic offerings to meet the growing demands in the healthcare and environmental sectors.
